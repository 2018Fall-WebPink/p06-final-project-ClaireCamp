Biography

Julie Ertz was born on April 6th, 1992. She was born and raised in Mesa, Arizona. Julie went to Dobson High School and Santa Clara University. She graduated with a communications degree. Julie is on the US Women's National Soccer Team. On the field, she is a defender. She was in the 2016 Summer Olympics. Her team placed 5th in the world. Her husband is Zach Ertz. He is the tight end for the Philadelphia Eagles. Zach and her wanted to create a foundation that made a difference in the world. Their new foundation is called the Ertz Family Foundation.

About the Ertz Family Foundation

The Ertz Family Foundation is run by Julie and Zach Ertz. This foundation was created by the idea that Julie and Zach wanted to use their athletic platform and they wanted to help as many people they could. Their foundation mainy focuses on the people in Philadelphia, PA, the Bay Area in California, and in Haiti. The idea of this foundation spark the minds of these two athletes because when Zach took a trip to Haiti and saw all the poor but joy in Haiti, he wanted to help them. The three pillars of the Ertz Family Foundation is to

  1. Bring the joy of sports to those who don't have them.
  2. Bring education to those who need it.
  3. Bring hope and joy to people and families who really need it in their lives.

So far the Ertz Family Foundation has raised $80,000 dollars for high school children in Haiti. This money will allow the kids to attend college. Julie Ertz explains, "We want to see these amazing kids reach their potential, achieve their dreams, and be all that God created them to be. Our foundation's vision is to transform lives through faith, hope, and love. This is an incredible opportunity to do just that." The Ertz Family Foundation's goal is to spread faith and hope to those who are need of it.
